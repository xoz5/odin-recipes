Vaselopita

Description

A greek cake traditionally eaten on New Year's Eve. A coin is placed inside prior to cooking, and whoever ends up with it gets 1 year of good luck!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 6 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 cup warm milk (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup blanched slivered almonds
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
